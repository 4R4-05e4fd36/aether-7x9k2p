## Reflections

[Anything sitting with you from today. Doesn't need to be profound - just honest. What are you thinking about? What felt hard? What felt good? What are you curious about?]

**Format notes:**
- Chronological is fine, but not required
- Bullet points work great
- Don't polish - just record
- The goal is EXTERNAL GROUND TRUTH, not impressive writing
- If you catch yourself revising to "look better," stop and write what actually happened
